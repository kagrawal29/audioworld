## Response Patterns

### What profiles respond:
- **Founders/Directors** respond best (Sanjay M, Satyam, Vivek, Sunny Jani all founders/directors)
- **Corporate Video segment** has best engagement so far
- **VO artists respond but as peers**, not buyers (Tanya, Lakshita, Rahul — inbound/peer dynamic)

### What doesn't work:
- **Plain CRs on free accounts** — 100% removal rate (tested Mar 4 batched, Mar 7 spread out)
- **Agency employees** (non-founders) — low response to DMs
- **Large agency contacts** (Netflix, Publicis, FCB) — CRs pending 10+ days, likely ignored

### Template effectiveness (early data):
- Personalized DMs referencing recent posts → 3/5 got replies (60%)
- Generic intro DMs → 1/3 got replies (33%)
- Comments on posts → builds visibility but doesn't directly convert

### Time patterns:
- Replies tend to come 1-3 days after DM
- After 5+ days of silence, lead is likely cold
- Follow-ups at 3-4 days may be too late — consider 2-day follow-up

## Drop Analysis

### Where leads stall:
1. **S1 → S2 (CR acceptance):** Biggest bottleneck. Plain CRs = dead. Only personalized CRs or incoming CRs progress.
2. **S3 → S4 (DM reply):** ~50% reply. Non-responders tend to be agency employees rather than founders.
3. **S4 → S5 (Meeting):** Nobody has progressed here yet. Need to actively push for meetings.

### Drop reasons:
- **S1 → Dead:** Plain CR removed by LinkedIn (50+ cases)
- **S1 → Cold:** CR pending 10+ days, likely ignored
- **S3 → Cold:** DM sent, no reply after 5+ days
- **S3 → Disconnected:** Lead may have removed connection (Vishal Yoman case)
- **S4 → CLOSED:** Explicitly declined (Suuresh Ramachandran)

## Predictive Signals

### High conversion probability:
- Founder/Director title
- Active poster on LinkedIn (creates content)
- Corporate Video or VO production company
- Responds to initial comment/like (reciprocal engagement)
- Inbound connection request

### Low conversion probability:
- Junior/mid-level agency employee
- Inactive on LinkedIn (no posts in 30 days)
- Large multinational agency (decision-making too distributed)
- No mutual connections

## Recommendations for Week 3+

1. **Focus on Corporate Video + Inbound** — highest conversion
2. **Deprioritize large agencies** for CRs (use comments/follows instead)
3. **Shorten follow-up window** from 3-4 days to 2 days
4. **Push S4 leads toward meetings aggressively** — Director Sanjay M needs a call TODAY
5. **Join LinkedIn groups** — potential source of inbound leads
6. **Personalize every DM** with post reference — 60% vs 33% reply rate
